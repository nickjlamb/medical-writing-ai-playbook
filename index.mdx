## AI-assisted medical writing workflow

AI supports specific steps in the content development pipeline. Expert human review remains essential at every stage — AI produces working material, humans verify and sign off.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Literature discovery" icon="microscope">
    Find and retrieve source papers, trial data, and prescribing information.
    [PubCrawl](/tools/pubcrawl) — search PubMed, trial registries, and SmPCs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Evidence analysis" icon="search">
    Summarise sources, extract key messages, and verify claims against references.
    [RefCheckr](/tools/refcheckr) — claim-to-reference verification
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drafting and structure" icon="file-text">
    Generate structured first drafts, content outlines, and deliverable frameworks from source materials and key messages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audience adaptation" icon="users">
    Adapt content for different audiences — specialists, GPs, nurses, payers, patients.
    [LLMentor](/tools/llmentor) — multi-audience adaptation | [Patiently AI](/tools/patiently-ai) — patient-friendly language
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compliance and verification" icon="shield">
    Pre-screen for promotional compliance signals and verify claims against cited references.
    [MedCheckr](/tools/medcheckr) — compliance screening | [RefCheckr](/tools/refcheckr) — reference verification
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final expert review" icon="user-check">
    A qualified medical writer or reviewer verifies accuracy, completeness, and compliance — then signs off. AI does not replace this step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Risk Tiers

Not all tasks carry the same consequences when AI is involved. Four tiers define the AI role, the review process, and what sign-off is required.

| Tier | AI role | Review required | Examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Low** | First draft, structuring | Standard medical writing review | Paper summaries, outlines, internal briefings |
| **Medium** | Transformation, adaptation | Enhanced review with source cross-check | Key messages, audience adaptation, repurposing |
| **High** | Limited drafting support | Expert review, full verification | Promotional claims, plain language summaries, compliance checks |
| **Critical** | Supporting role only | Full expert review, formal sign-off | Final QC before client delivery or publication |
