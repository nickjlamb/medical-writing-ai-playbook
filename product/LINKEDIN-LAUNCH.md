**Post:**

We've published the Medical Writing AI Playbook — a free, open resource for using AI safely in medical writing workflows.

It's not a prompt library. It's a structured framework covering where AI helps, where it creates risk, and what humans must still do at every step.

What's inside:

→ 10 workflow cards covering the full content development pipeline — from source paper summarisation to pre-MLR compliance checks
→ A risk tier framework (Low / Medium / High / Critical) with concrete examples of what goes wrong at each level
→ 20+ prompt patterns designed for specific med comms tasks, not generic AI use
→ Review checklists, failure modes, and human sign-off requirements for every workflow
→ Integration points showing where purpose-built tools fit into each workflow step

Built for:
• Freelance and in-house medical writers
• Med comms agencies standardising AI use across teams
• Pharma brand, medical affairs, and training teams evaluating AI practices

Two principles run through every workflow:
AI for acceleration, not authority.
Translation, not invention.

The playbook is completely free. No login, no gating, no subscription.
